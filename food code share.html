The food we eat can affect what we think, feel and do. Nutrition is therefore just as important to know about as everything else to do with mental health. Bad diets/unbalanced diets have been linked to bad academic performance, depression, anxiety, ADHD, schizophrenia and dementia. Simply being mindful of what you are eating can help you to maintain a healthy diet, videos that could help you with your general diet are linked on this page.
