Fitting regular exercise into your routine has been found to have a positive impact on many factors that can impact your mental health. When you exercise your body produces more of its natural mood elevators – endorphins – and reduces levels of stress hormones like cortisol. So, exercising can promote more positive moods and supports stress management. Take a look at this video to learn more about why exercising can help you to improve your mental health.
